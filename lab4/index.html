Making a Reduction Wood Cut:

  1. Sketch out your drawing
    • Decided where your layers will be within your piece
  2. Use tracing paper to transfer your drawing to the wood panel
  3. Carve out your first layer (what you carve will be white)
  4. Choose and prep your paper
  5. Decide on your first color
    • Find the relief ink
    • Mix to your liking
  6. Roll up the wood panel
    • Spread a band of ink on the table
    • Roll it out with the brayer
  7. Set the pressure of the printing press
  8. Print your first layer
    • Put newsprint and blotter paper to protect the press from getting dirty
  9. Do some ghost prints to remove excess ink
  10. Carve your next layer
  11. Wait until the ink is dry on your prints before repeating the printing process with a new ink color
  12. Repeat until satisfied
  13. Clean your tools and station when finished

How to Code Flappy Bird:

  1. Print start screen
  2. Allow user input
  3. The user clicks a button to start the game
  4. Mouse click makes the bird fly through obstacles
    • The bird falls by itself when nothing happens
  5. Clicking makes the bird fly upward
  6. Randomize the pathway in each game
  7. Count the number of successful passes
  8. If the bird crashes you lose the game!
    • Print game over screen
  9. Record data of high score for the user
